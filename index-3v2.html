Our Holiday Lighting Process

Our all-inclusive Christmas light installation service at Northwest Pro Exteriors is designed to make holiday decorating effortless and enjoyable for you. Here is our simple process.

Consultation & Design

We start by discussing your vision for your home’s holiday lights. Whether you’re looking for a classic warm white look or a colorful display, we create a design that fits your home perfectly.

High-Quality Lights Provided

We own the lights, so you don’t need to purchase or store them. Our lights are professional-grade, ensuring durability and a beautiful, vibrant display all season long.

Professional Installation

Our team handles the entire installation with safety and precision, paying attention to every detail to make sure the lights look stunning and are securely in place.

In Season Maintenance

If any issues arise during the season, such as a strand coming loose, we’ll take care of it at no additional charge. This $0 maintenance service ensures your lights stay looking perfect throughout the holidays.

End-of-Season Takedown

When the season ends, we’ll return to safely remove the lights and store them until next year, so you don’t have to worry about any cleanup or storage.
