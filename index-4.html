Welcome

We've Got Your Christmas Lights Covered!

Northwest Pro Exteriors is a trusted home improvement company specializing in high-quality Christmas light installations. With our all-inclusive holiday lighting service, we bring festive beauty and cheer to homes throughout the Pacific Northwest. Our team designs displays that perfectly suit each home, using our own premium lights for a customized, hassle-free experience.

Our service includes installation, in-season maintenance, and takedown after the season, so homeowners can enjoy the holidays without any stress. Whether you prefer classic warm white or colorful patterns, we create stunning displays that make your home shine all season long.

With years of experience in exterior services, Northwest Pro Exteriors ensures a professional, safe, and reliable holiday lighting experience for your home.
